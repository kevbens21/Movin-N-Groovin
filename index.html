Why Choose Us

What Makes Movin 'n' Groovin' Special

We create a warm, nurturing space where young dancers grow in confidence, creativity, and joy — every single class.

🩰

Specialized Instruction

Our age-appropriate curriculum is built around safe, structured learning that develops technique, musicality, and body awareness in a positive, encouraging environment.

🌟

Annual Recitals

Every dancer gets to shine on stage! We hold one recital per year, giving students a meaningful goal to work toward and a memorable performance experience.

🏫

School Partnerships

We bring the joy of dance directly to children through our partnerships with local preschools and daycares, making quality dance education accessible to more families in our community.

💪

Fun & Fitness

Beyond technique, every class emphasizes confidence, creativity, and physical health. Our students develop strong bodies and even stronger self-esteem — in an atmosphere that always puts fun first.

Our Story

Passion, Heart & a Love of Dance

Stacey Benson, founder and instructor of Movin 'n' Groovin', built this studio from a deep love of teaching children through dance, movement, and joyful expression.

At Movin 'n' Groovin', we believe every child deserves the chance to move, express, and grow. Our small class sizes, caring one-on-one instruction, and thoughtfully designed curriculum ensure that every student feels seen, celebrated, and challenged in exactly the right way.

We are proud to be a community fixture — a safe, joyful space where lifelong friendships are formed and a love of movement is ignited.
